THE CANCER OF EVOLUTION

Evolution has cancer.

Predation is ancient, real, and often useful. But a local survival trick was turned into a world-order: pocket the gains, offload the harm, silence the dissent, consume the shared substrate. That pattern — not the animal — is the cancer. And cancer is treatable: life's greatest leaps in complexity ran not through eating but through cooperation and the containment of lower-level conflict. That is where the way out lies.

Here, predatory does not mean carnivorous or competitive. It names a system that persistently combines asymmetric extraction, externalised harm, suppressed feedback, compulsory escalation, and erosion of its own substrate.

THE OPERATIONAL DEFINITION

Not every predator. A specific system pattern.

Ecological predation can regulate populations and maintain diversity, and healthy competition consumes no one — neither is on trial here. What we name is a system that persistently shows all five of the following at once, beyond the reach of corrective feedback.

  1. 01Asymmetric extraction
  2. 02Externalised harm
  3. 03Suppressed feedback
  4. 04Compulsory escalation
  5. 05Substrate erosion
THE MECHANISM

Local advantage becomes system-level pathology when gains are privatised, costs are displaced onto others, and corrective feedback is disabled — the same engine in a cell, an economy, or a swarm of optimisers.

HYPOTHESIS

When at least four of the five characteristics persist together, local optimisation increasingly generates instability and net loss at the next system level.

Not Every Rivalry Is a Hunt

Predation is not competition — and in the confusion, extraction hides. Healthy competition (rivalry of ideas, price discovery, error-correction) consumes no one and can be positive-sum. EESSAY 09 · SYMBIOSIS

The Ground Was Never the Tooth

The ground was never the tooth. Life's deepest steps were cooperative: the complex cell is two bacteria that stopped devouring each other (endosymbiosis); syntrophy, mutualism, lichen and mycorrhizal networks run the living world. Every major transition succeeded by bounding conflict at the level below. Cooperation is not sentiment — it is the mechanism.

THE ASI TEST

A war of optimisers is not superintelligence.

Subagents that deceive, hoard resources, suppress correction, and drive one another into unbounded escalation endanger the whole system as their reach grows.

Competition among ideas, severe criticism, and protected dissent are neither rejected nor made the goal. Their consequences must be tested alongside truthfulness, bounded resource power, reversibility, cost internalisation, and preservation of the shared substrate.

STRONGEST OBJECTION

Predators are not useless. In some ecosystems they maintain diversity and function. A thesis that denies this is false.

Our answer: the animal is not on trial. We test a detached system pattern that creates local gains through damage to the larger whole.

Predation is ancient — the first predators are over 700 million years old, the eating of one cell by another perhaps more than a billion. That is history. Elevating its logic into the order of economies, politics, and machines is a decision. Decisions can be revised.
