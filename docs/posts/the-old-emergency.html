A predator is not a model for the future

An ancient survival strategy was elevated into a worldview.

Predation exists and can perform functions in present ecosystems. This creates no obligation to copy its logic into economies, politics, or artificial intelligence.

The Predator Principle begins when extraction externalises harm, disables correction, compels escalation, and damages the shared substrate. What wins locally can make the larger system lose.
