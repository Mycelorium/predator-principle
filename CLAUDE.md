# Claude repository instructions

Preserve the project's two-layer voice: provocative thesis plus auditable claims.

Before merging any content change:

1. Keep every factual public claim linked to a claim ID.
2. Do not silently promote `I` or `P` to `E`.
3. Prefer primary research; register counterevidence in the same change.
4. Never describe the entire scientific field as corrupt or captured without
   reproducible evidence. Treat training-data and framing bias as hypotheses.
5. Do not equate cooperation with obedience or dissent with predation.
6. The 24 items in `content/campaign.json` are explicitly authorised by the
   Nirodha Collective launch mandate. Do not add new items to that mandate or
   auto-approve material from the research inbox.
7. Run `node scripts/validate.mjs` before committing.
8. Treat competition as neither the goal nor harmful per se; assess whether its
   actual effects meet the five Predator Principle criteria.

The files under `fire_work/` are private background material and must never be
added to this repository.
