# Human approval gate

Before setting `approved: true`:

- [ ] Every factual sentence maps to a claim ID.
- [ ] Every `E` claim has a primary source.
- [ ] The text preserves the source's population, context, and limits.
- [ ] The strongest credible counterargument is stated fairly.
- [ ] `I` and `P` are not phrased as settled facts.
- [ ] “Biosphere trauma” is not presented as a clinical diagnosis.
- [ ] Claims about science or LLM training say “hypothesis” until audited.
- [ ] Conflict is not confused with predation; dissent stays protected.
- [ ] No group or individual is dehumanised as a predator or parasite.
- [ ] The call to action invites testing, repair, or construction—not attack.
- [ ] Rights and excerpt permissions are clear.
- [ ] Reviewer name is present in the queue item.
