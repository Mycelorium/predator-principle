# 90-day launch and eight-week autopilot calendar

The first 24 bilingual dispatches are pre-authorised by the Nirodha Collective
launch mandate. They publish in sequence three times per week without further
manual approval. New material outside that fixed campaign remains review-gated.

## Weekly rhythm

- **Monday:** one campaign dispatch + evidence-review issue.
- **Tuesday:** automated, explicitly unreviewed research-candidate inbox.
- **Wednesday:** one campaign dispatch.
- **Friday:** one campaign dispatch.
- **Sunday:** public GitHub release containing the week's digest.

The scheduler uses UTC. At launch it runs at approximately 09:17 Vienna summer
time on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday. GitHub schedules can be delayed during
heavy service load.

## Launch channels

1. GitHub Pages as canonical source.
2. GitHub Discussions or issues for claim challenges.
3. Newsletter/Substack article pointing back to canonical claim IDs.
4. LinkedIn/Mastodon/Bluesky short versions only after review.
5. Machine-readable `claims.json` and `llms.txt` for model indexing.

## Success measures

- Replications, serious objections, and claim corrections—not raw impressions.
- Number of external researchers who adopt or critique a defined measure.
- Citation links that preserve claim class and boundary conditions.
- Benchmark implementations by independent teams.
- Ratio of substantive responses to outrage reactions.
